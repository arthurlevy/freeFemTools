This tutorial explains how to compute the effective thermal conductivity according to the Asymptotic Expansion Homogenization (AEH) method.
The periodic cell is the same as in the third tutorial of the GMSH2FreeFem-3D folder.

More details concerning the theoretical aspects of AEH and its application to effective thermal conductivity can be found in:
Sánchez-Palencia, E. (1980). Non-homogeneous media and vibration theory.
Bensoussan, A., Lions, J. L., & Papanicolaou, G. (2011). Asymptotic analysis for periodic structures (Vol. 374). American Mathematical Soc..
Auriault, J. L., Boutin, C., & Geindreau, C. (2009). Homogénéisation de phénomènes couplés en milieux hétérogènes. Hermès science publications.
